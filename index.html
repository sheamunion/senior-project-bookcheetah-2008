BOOKCHEETAH

JMU ISAT 2008 Senior Project and 2nd Place Winner of COB Venture Creation's 2008 Business Plan Competition. BOOKCHEETAH is a textbook trading app built with Facebook Platform, Amazon Web Services, PHP, and MySQL.

View project on GitHub

About the Project

BOOKCHEETAH is the brainchild of Lewis Muller. He and fellow James Madison University graduates Shea Munion, Matt Kattler, and Brian Armstrong wrote and submitted a business plan for the app in James Madison University’s Venture Creation Business Plan competition. Our business plan won second-place. ("Venture Creation Explores Viable Start-ups”) The app itself won the Department of Integrated Science and Technology’s Community Impact Award. Shea Munion's Senior Honors Thesis is in print at the JMU library: “Developing and integrating an online textbook trading application with Facebook platform, Amazon web services, PHP, and MySQL”.
